This Work

We are all consumers, be it of human behaviour, lifestyle, fashion, the arts or technology. We have a responsibility to respond to this.

Through visual means; sampling, remixing, criticising, sympathising, experimenting, the work aims to be critical, challenging and relevant to today's culture.

About Luke

From a print background; achieving a BA in Design for Publishing at NUA and a Masters degree in Communication Design. During that time, fine art techniques and a critical edge began to influence the work.

Currently employed by Selesti, of Norwich, England as their Digital Designer with a multitude of clients on web, tablet and mobile platforms.
