Make with feeling™

I'm fascinated with consumerism. It fits into everything I make.

The Work

Whether it''s culture, fashion, lifestyles, media, technology or even people's words and actions, we are all consumers. As a designer, I feel that I have a responsibility to respond to this.

Through visual means, sampling, remixing, criticising, sympathising, experimenting, I want to produce work that is critical, challenging and relevant to today's culture.

About Luke

From a print background; achieving a BA in Design for Publishing at NUA, I then moved onto study Communication Design at MA level. During this time, I discovered that fine art techniques and a critical edge enabled me to grow creatively and push my ideas further.

I am currently working at Selesti in Norwich as a Digital Designer with a multitude of clients on web, tablet and mobile platforms.
